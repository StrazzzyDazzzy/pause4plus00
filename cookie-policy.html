What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work efficiently, remember your preferences, and provide information to the site owner.

Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies — needed for core site functionality, such as navigation, security, and basic performance.
  • Performance & Analytics Cookies — help us understand how visitors use the Site so we can improve content and user experience.
  • Functionality Cookies — remember choices you make on the Site to provide a more personalized experience.
  • Third-Party Cookies — set by service providers we rely on, such as content delivery and security (Cloudflare) and font hosting (Google Fonts), to support Site performance and design.

How We Use Cookies

We use cookies to operate and secure the Site, understand how visitors interact with our pages, improve our content and services, and remember basic preferences during your visit.

Your Choices

Most web browsers let you control cookies through their settings, including deleting existing cookies and blocking new ones. Please note that disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality or appearance of the Site. Check your browser's help resources for instructions specific to your browser.

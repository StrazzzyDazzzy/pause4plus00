My Story

Aaron Strazicich

Founder — Pause 4 Plus LLC

Aaron brings over 12 years of experience in technology, revenue operations, and leadership across high-growth organizations.

During his career he helped scale companies from $3M to over $40M in monthly recurring revenue while leading teams of over 100 people and consistently achieving top 1% performance results.

He founded Pause 4 Plus to share the knowledge, systems, and discipline that fueled his own success, empowering others to build lasting financial freedom of their own.

Aaron is driven by a mission to build companies that create opportunity, strengthen communities, and give back to underserved kids and families.

Outside of work he enjoys spending time with his family, surfing, and playing tennis.
